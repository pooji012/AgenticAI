Refund Policy
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of delivery if the item is unused, returned in original packaging, or confirmed damaged on arrival.

Shipping Policy
Standard delivery usually takes 3 to 5 business days after dispatch. If tracking has not updated for more than 72 hours, support should contact the logistics team.

Damaged Product Policy
For damaged or defective products, customers should provide order ID, product name, and a photo. Support may offer a replacement or refund after review.

Billing Policy
Duplicate charges, chargebacks, suspected fraud, or payment disputes should be escalated to senior support or billing specialists.
